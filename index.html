My Workflow

Step 1: Identity problem(s), hypothesize, and create empathy

The discovery phase where we explore problems, list out assumptions and questions, build empathy (explore user habits, lifestyle, goals) and define success/failure metrics for testing.

Step 2: Build MVP

The phase where we build something to validate our ideas. This is where tools like Sketch or Axure come in handy!

Step 3: Test, analyze results and reiterate

Arguably the most important step in every workfow. After examining user data and feedback, ideas are either validated or invalidated and new ideas arise. The workflow then begins again! :)
