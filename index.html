My Workflow

Step 1: Identity problem(s), hypothesize, and create empathy

In this phase we list out assumptions and questions, build empathy, and define success and failure metrics for testing.

Step 2: Build MVP

The phase where we build the minimum viable product to validate our ideas. This is where tools like Sketch or Axure come in handy!

Step 3: Test, analyze results and reiterate

After examining user data and feedback, ideas are either validated or invalidated and new ideas arise. The workflow then begins again! :)

More About Me

In my current position I work closely with product leadership to help design solutions, produce and maintain technical documentation, manage our design system, create test cases for QA, and help prioritize features for our product roadmap.
