My Workflow

For most of my career I have utilized methodologies from Lean UX and Google Venture Design Sprints with my teams. As every problem is different, the workflow we use may change but what follows is my main baseline applied to a case study for an app to help user adapt healthy eating habits.

Step 1: Identify problem(s), hypothesize, and create empathy

In this case study I focused on the main problem of unhealthy eating habits. I started out by listing all related problems, assumptions about behaviors, key success metrics, and product features. The following are main takeaways from this step.


HYPOTHESIS: Increased personal accountability, community engagement, and incremental changes will lead users to make healthier eating choices.

HOW: Users with the same goals engage in community discord, participate in weekly challenges; ability to communicate with a certified nutritionist and ability to report and help fight cravings.

I"m also mindful of what the 'hook' will be for the platfom to become a regular habit for the user since the app relies on continous engagement to be successful.
