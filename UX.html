Welcome Pack: The Coca-Cola Company

AB Testing | Interviews

The Welcome Pack guides new hires for Coke to relevant onboarding information. My team was tasked with discovering which variant of the Welcome Pack navigation increased conversions. To do this we conducted AB testing and interviews to collect qualitative data. We then presented our recommendation based on our findings.

PAC Place

Wireframing | Interviews | Web Design

PAC is a global internal team that needed one central location for employees to find relevant documents and updates. I was tasked with interviewing employees and stakeholders to understand what problems that PAC Place could solve for them and discover some of their daily challenges with communicating and collaborating globally. After collecting requirements I created wireframes and a working protoype on web to illustrate the PAC Place solution.
