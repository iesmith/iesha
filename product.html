Diasyst: Diabetes Simplified

Interface Design | Prototyping | User Flows | Design Systems

Diasyst is a diabetes management platform for providers and their patients. Diasyst's mission is to solve the problem of clinical inertia. As product designer I helped conceptualize and design out complex workflows for the provider and patient app. I designed components, modals, interactive prototypes and user flows.

Diasyst's Design System

"If it isn't documented, it doesn't exist"

Once a solid product was established, Diasyt needed a central location for our company guidelines, style guide, design components, sketch files, and product documentation to help unify our entire product team.
I audited all of our screens, created a UI Inventory, organized everything into a skeleton in Google Docs, and wrote out all product requirements for our web and mobile app in Confluence. After establishing our system, I worked to maintain and keep everything updated with each new release.
